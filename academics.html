Elementary and Junior High School

Academics

The Basic Education department of Arellano University offers a structured learning environment from Pre-School to Grade 10, ensuring students develop the necessary foundational skills before proceeding to specialized tracks in Senior High.

Elementary Department

Elementary Program

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and character development.

  • Primary Levels (Grades 1-3)
  • Intermediate Levels (Grades 4-6)
Junior High School

High School Program

Transitioning students to more complex academic disciplines and social responsibility.

  • Grades 7 to 10
  • Science and Technology Focus
  • Extracurricular and Leadership Training

Arellano University's Senior High School program offers a diverse range of tracks and strands designed to prepare students for college life, professional careers, and entrepreneurial ventures.

Academic Track

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

Tailored for students aiming for careers in medicine, engineering, pure sciences, and technology. The curriculum emphasizes advanced mathematics, calculus, and scientific research methodologies.
